28-Bar Piano Practice Plan (2-bar chunks, backward chaining)

Fully specified day-by-day plan for learning the entire piece in 14 days, then consolidating. Chunk numbering is from the end: Chunk 1 = bars 27–28 … Chunk 14 = bars 1–2.

Chunk size: 2 bars
Total chunks: 14
New chunks/day: 1
Recall: mandatory
Binding: mandatory

Daily structure (same order every day)

1
Recall phase — all previously learned chunks (2–3 reps each).
2
Acquisition phase — today’s new chunk (25 reps; Day 1: 25–30).
3
Repair phase — only if recall failed (5–10 reps targeted; stop once recall works).
4
Binding phase — connect chunks exactly as listed for the day.
Chunk map:
  • Chunk 1 = bars 27–28
  • Chunk 2 = bars 25–26
  • …
  • Chunk 14 = bars 1–2

Non-negotiable rules

  • If recall fails → repair immediately (do not “save it for later”).
  • Never skip recall to “save time.”
  • Do not increase reps just because you feel uncertain; fix the defect instead.
  • Continuity training (chains/runs) is diagnostic; do not stop during those runs.
Implementation detail: during Recall, play at a tempo that allows correctness without hesitating. If it collapses, mark that chunk for Repair.
